Who We Are

Exhibition Solutions, established in 2012 and headquartered in New Delhi, is a trusted name in providing cutting-edge exhibition services across India. We specialize in offering end-to-end solutions for events, ensuring that every aspect of your exhibition is flawlessly executed. From designing and setting up booths to providing technical support and advanced technology for seamless presentations, we focus on creating engaging and memorable experiences. Our expert team, with a rich background in engineering and technology, is dedicated to delivering high-quality services tailored to the specific needs of each event. At Exhibition Solutions, we help businesses effectively showcase their products and services, making every event a success.
